Olivia's Horse Show Season Fundraiser

Kentucky Derby Countdown: 19d 1h 10m 49s

Game Overview

  • 10×10 grid = 100 squares
  • $10 per square
  • $500 to the winner
  • $500 goes towards show season
  • Once full, numbers (0–9) are randomly assigned to rows and columns

How to Join

  • Pick your square(s) on the grid below
  • Fill out the form that pops up
  • Venmo @Magen-Newman-1 $10 per square or pay with cash
  • Add a 🐎 emoji and your email in the Venmo note

How to Win

  • Winning square is based on the last digit of the horses' numbers
  • Columns = 🥇 1st place finishers
  • Rows = 🥈 2nd place finishers
  • Example: If horse #12 wins 🥇 1st and #4 comes 🥈 2nd → square (2, 4) wins

Multiple Ways to Win

Your square can win in 4 different ways:

For square (2, 4), you'd win if:

  • 🥇 Horse #2 and 🥈 Horse #4
  • 🥇 Horse #2 and 🥈 Horse #14
  • 🥇 Horse #12 and 🥈 Horse #4
  • 🥇 Horse #12 and 🥈 Horse #14
